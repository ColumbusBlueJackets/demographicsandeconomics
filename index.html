Stats & Figures behind America's Boom Town

The right time to settle down in Columbus is NOW!
Need proof? We'll let the numbers speak for themselves...

BIG

  • The 14th-largest city in the U.S. and the second-largest in the Midwest, behind Chicago​.
  • The eleventh-fastest-growing large city in the country.
  • Columbus metro area is No. 1 in the Midwest for population, job and GDP growth.
  • Ohio State and Number of students and where it stands in USA as largest university.

SMART

  • More than 50 college and university campuses with over 133,000 enrolled students​.
  • Top ranked K-12 schools including 9 public and private high schools in the US top 500 US News and Niche.com, including 6 public K-12 districts in the U.S. top 250.​
  • Top 10 Smart Cities in the U.S.

THRIVING

  • Home to 17 Fortune 1000 headquarters, including 6 Fortune 500s​.
  • Diverse and durable economy, including hubs in the advanced computing, automotive & mobility, e-commerce, finance & insurance, and biomedical industries​.
  • Top 10 rising cities for startups / Top 20 best US cities for startups​.

DIVERSE

  • More than 90 different languages are spoken in the homes of area K-12 students.​
  • 37% of population growth in the metro area is from international migration​.
  • Due to demographics, location and retail headquarters, Columbus is the nation’s renowned test market for retail.

AFFORDABLE

  • With a cost of living nearly 10% below the national average, Columbus offers an affordable market for working professionals.​
  • 71% of home sold are affordable to the area median income.​
  • No. 1 in the Midwest and No. 12 in the U.S. among large metros for millennial migration.

ACCESSIBLE

  • Within 500 miles of nearly half of the U.S. population​.
  • 15-minute average commute from downtown international airport​.
